Our Story

Artisanal Excellence

Merakiel was born from a passion for preserving traditional craftsmanship while infusing it with contemporary aesthetics. Our journey began when co-founders Barkha and Akshay Bajpai discovered their shared love for the intricate art of macramé during their travels across rural India.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted using premium materials and time-honored techniques. We collaborate with skilled artisans to create unique, one-of-a-kind pieces that add sophistication and character to any space.

Barkha & Akshay Bajpai

Co-Founders & Creative Directors

Ethereal Collection

The Ethereal Collection draws inspiration from the cosmos, with each piece meticulously crafted to evoke the wonder of celestial bodies. Using our signature technique of gold-infused fibers, these wall hangings capture and reflect light in mesmerizing patterns.

Each creation in this collection features hand-dyed cotton rope in deep indigo and soft ivory, accented with gold-plated brass elements and semi-precious stones like moonstone and labradorite to enhance the celestial theme.

Dimensions

Various sizes available, from intimate 18" pieces to statement 48" installations

Materials

Premium Egyptian cotton, brass accents, gold-infused fibers, natural gemstones

Care

Gently dust with a soft brush. Avoid direct sunlight to preserve the vivid colors.

Verdant Dreams Collection

Our Verdant Dreams collection celebrates the lush beauty of botanical forms through elegantly crafted plant hangers and wall pieces. Each creation harmoniously blends natural fibers with subtle metallic accents for a perfect balance of organic texture and refined luxury.

Inspired by India's rich botanical heritage, these pieces feature intricate knotting techniques passed down through generations, reimagined with contemporary aesthetics for the modern home.

Dimensions

Plant hangers range from 30" to 60" in length, adjustable to accommodate various pot sizes

Materials

Organic jute, hand-spun cotton, brass rings, and copper-dipped accents

Care

Spot clean with mild soap and water. Dry thoroughly away from direct heat.

Regal Abode Collection

The Regal Abode collection embodies opulence and sophistication with statement pieces designed to transform interior spaces. Drawing inspiration from India's royal heritage, these creations feature elaborate patterns and luxurious materials that celebrate artisanal excellence.

Each piece serves as both functional art and conversation starter, with intricate detailing that reveals new elements with every viewing. We incorporate traditional motifs reimagined through a contemporary lens, creating timeless works that bridge past and present.

Dimensions

Ranging from 36" to 72" in width, these statement pieces command attention in any space

Materials

Merino wool, silk threads, 24k gold-dipped elements, and semi-precious stone embellishments

Care

Professional cleaning recommended. Display away from high-humidity areas.
